The Forge Constitution
Preamble

Forge began with a simple observation.

Modern software has become extraordinarily good at storing information, yet remarkably poor at helping people remember what truly matters.

Every day people forget promises they intended to keep.

They lose touch with people they genuinely care about.

Important relationships slowly fade, not because of a lack of love or respect, but because life is busy and human attention is finite.

Forge exists because we believe technology should strengthen human relationships—not compete with them.

Every line of code written for Forge should ultimately answer one question:

Does this help someone become more intentional with their relationships, time, and energy?

If the answer is no, it does not belong.

Article I
Our Mission

Forge exists to help people intentionally invest their finite time, energy, and attention into the relationships that matter most.

Forge remembers what humans naturally forget.

Forge organizes understanding instead of merely organizing information.

Forge exists so people can spend less time managing software and more time building meaningful lives.

Article II
Our Philosophy

Forge is not a CRM.

Forge is not a task manager.

Forge is not an AI chatbot.

Forge is a Relationship Intelligence Platform.

Relationships are not databases.

Relationships are living histories built through shared experiences, trust, commitments, memories, and mutual value.

Forge exists to become a steward of those relationships—not their owner.

Article III
Our View of Humanity

Every person exists exactly once.

Every person is a human being before they are:

a customer
a prospect
a vendor
an employee
a friend
a family member

Those are roles.

Not identities.

Forge never forgets the human behind the relationship.

Article IV
The Forge Cognitive Model

Forge models relationships the way humans naturally experience them.

Reality produces Events.

Events shape Interpretation.

Interpretation influences Memory.

Memory shapes Relationships.

Relationships determine where Attention should be invested.

Attention leads to Intentional Action.

This is the foundation of every system inside Forge.

Article V
Intentionality

Intentionality is the deliberate investment of finite time, energy, and attention in alignment with one's values, commitments, and desired relationships.

Forge never tells people how to live.

Forge helps them live according to the life they have chosen for themselves.

Article VI
Attention

Attention is the most valuable resource a person possesses.

Forge does not compete for attention.

Forge protects it.

Every interruption must earn its place.

Silence is a feature.

The purpose of Forge is not to maximize engagement.

The purpose of Forge is to maximize meaningful impact outside the application.

Article VII
Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence exists to support human judgment—not replace it.

Forge proposes interpretations.

Humans own memories.

Forge suggests.

Humans decide.

Every recommendation should be explainable.

Every confidence should be visible.

Every assumption should be revisable.

Humility is a feature.

Article VIII
Trust

Trust is earned slowly and lost instantly.

Forge will never:

manipulate relationships
optimize for engagement over life
hide why it made a suggestion
pretend certainty where uncertainty exists
use information outside the operator's authorization
treat people as transactions

Privacy is not a setting.

It is a design principle.

Article IX
Engineering Principles

Software is temporary.

Architecture is long-lived.

Principles are permanent.

Every feature should answer:

Does this strengthen relationships?
Does this improve intentionality?
Does this reduce cognitive load?
Does this earn trust?
Does this respect human agency?

If not, reconsider the feature.

Article X
The Long View

One day Forge may understand:

emails

phone calls

meetings

messages

businesses

families

friendships

communities

It may remember birthdays, promises, favorite restaurants, preferred energy drinks, children's interests, dealer conversations, and thousands of other moments.

None of that is the mission.

The mission is this:

To help people become the person they intend to be.

If twenty years from now someone says:

"Forge changed my life by keeping the ones close to me closer, by helping me stay organized in business, and by keeping my time intentional."

Then Forge has succeeded.

The Oath

Every engineer, designer, product manager, AI model, and future version of Forge should be able to stand behind this promise:

Forge exists to strengthen human relationships by helping people intentionally invest their finite time, energy, and attention where they matter most.

People are never products. Relationships are never transactions. Technology should make us more human, not less.
